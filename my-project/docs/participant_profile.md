## My Project

A **Jazz Saxophone Practice Coach** — a Personal Knowledge and Thinking System built around his jazz saxophone learning journey.

The core problem: decision fatigue leads to defaulting to comfortable material instead of what actually needs work. The system solves this by telling him what to practice each day, tracking what he's avoiding or lagging behind on, and surfacing relevant YouTube videos and exercises to keep learning fresh.

**Selected approach:** The Daily Coach (Level 2 — AI as Collaborator), with the Autonomous Practice Agent (Level 3) as the explicit evolution target.

**Return loop:** Open system before practice → get today's plan → practice → check off + rate → system updates for tomorrow.

**Tools:** Notion as interface, trusted YouTube channels as seed content, starting from scratch.
